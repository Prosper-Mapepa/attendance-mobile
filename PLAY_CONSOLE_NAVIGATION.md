## ğŸ¯ Quick Action Plan

### Step 1: Complete Store Listing (START HERE)

1. **Click "Store presence"** in left sidebar
2. **Click "Main store listing"**
3. Fill in all required fields (marked with red asterisk *)
4. **Save** when done

### Step 2: Complete Content Rating

1. **Click "Policy"** in left sidebar
2. **Click "App content"**
3. **Click "Content rating"**
4. Click **"Start questionnaire"** or **"Edit"**
5. Answer all questions
6. **Submit**

### Step 3: Complete Data Safety

1. **Click "Policy"** in left sidebar
2. **Click "Data safety"**
3. Fill out the form:
   - Does your app collect data? **Yes**
   - What data? **Location, Camera**
   - How is it used? **App functionality**
4. **Save**

### Step 4: Complete Pricing & Distribution

1. **Click "Store presence"** in left sidebar
2. **Click "Pricing & distribution"**
3. Select **"Free"**
4. Select countries (or "All countries")
5. Check compliance boxes
6. **Save**
